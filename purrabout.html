PURRfect Space Handmade is my little piece of the world, where I share an ever changing collection of unique ceramic home décor, handcrafted dinnerware and quirky jewellery made in East London.
I love creating small scale, functional, contemporary designs and clay is a fascinating medium to work, with almost endless possibilities. While I work mainly in ceramics I also experiment with metalworking, sewing and weaving.


Why Handmade Matters

Handmade is purely passion made and it’s wonderful that more people everywhere are beginning to embrace things that are made by hand in small quantities. Each handcrafted product represents the dedication, expertise of the artisan and precious uniqueness that is rare these days and can never be replaced by a machine.
Handmade items are crafted in an environment of joy and respect which makes them more environmentally sustainable. I believe that each handcrafted product with the quality and craftsmanship put into work can be used and loved for many years to come.
